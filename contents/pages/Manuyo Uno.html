Manuyo Uno, Las Pinas City

As we continue to conquer the COVID-19 pandemic, Alltimetech Solutions Inc. strives to help the country’s growing unemployment problem. The company launches its 2nd WeTap Kapartner Mo, a program that aims to assist the Barangay’s livelihood program.

With the utmost support of Barangay Chairman Garry Riggs of Manuyo Uno, Las Pinas City, Alltimetech Solutions Inc. marked its 2nd Barangay Training Program last March 13, 2021.
